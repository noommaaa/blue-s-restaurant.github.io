About Us

Welcome to Blue's

Welcome to Blue's Restaurant, where the culinary journey takes you on a captivating voyage through flavors inspired by the deep blue sea. Nestled in the heart of coastal elegance, our restaurant is a celebration of the ocean's bounty, crafted with a passion for exceptional dining. At Blue's, we believe in the artistry of gastronomy, blending the finest ingredients with culinary expertise to create a symphony of tastes and textures. Our chefs, led by the visionary Chef Lipho, draw inspiration from the ocean's embrace, curating a menu that reflects the beauty and diversity of the underwater world.

Menu

Blue Ocean Salad

Fresh mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and avocado tossed in a zesty lemon vinaigrette. Topped with grilled shrimp and blue cheese crumbles.

Seaside Pasta Delight

Linguine pasta served with a flavorful seafood medley of clams, mussels, and shrimp in a garlic white wine sauce. Garnished with fresh parsley and a hint of lemon.

Oceanic Grilled Salmon

Grilled Atlantic salmon fillet glazed with a honey-mustard and dill sauce. Served on a bed of quinoa pilaf with sautéed spinach.

Black and Blue Tuna Steak

Pan-seared blackened tuna steak crusted with Cajun spices, topped with a blue cheese and herb compound butter. Accompanied by roasted sweet potatoes.

Mango Tango Shrimp Tacos

Soft corn tortillas filled with chili-lime marinated shrimp, mango salsa, shredded cabbage, and a drizzle of cilantro-lime crema. Served with a side of black bean and corn salad.

Silver Wave Chocolate Mousse

A decadent chocolate mousse dessert with layers of dark and white chocolate, topped with silver-dusted edible pearls. Served in a martini glass for a touch of elegance.
